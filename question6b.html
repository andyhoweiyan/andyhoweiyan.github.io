Recipe Chicken Curry

  • 800 g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180 g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves


Cooking Steps

  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant
  2. Add in water,tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt
  3. Simmer in slow fire until potatotes becomes soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly
  4. Add in coconut milk
  5. Cook until oil starts to separate
  6. Remove and serve
